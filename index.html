How do I contact customer support for assistance?

Our dedicated support team is available 24/7. You can reach us via phone, email, or the live chat feature on our website for prompt assistance.

Can I choose specific elements for my modular kitchen or wardrobe design?

Absolutely! We encourage personalization. During the consultation, discuss your preferences with our design team, and we'll tailor the modular kitchen, wardrobe, or any other element to match your unique style and requirements.

Are your electrical and carpentry services available on a one-time basis, or can I opt for ongoing maintenance plans?

We offer both one-time services and flexible maintenance plans. Whether you need a quick fix or long-term support, our team is equipped to meet your specific requirements. Explore our service packages for cost-effective solutions tailored to your needs.
